Allow your visitor/customer submit instant visual feedback on your website.

== Description ==

Feedback Modal for Website is the best ways to collect visitor/customer feedback on your website. Getting feedback helps you identify and eliminate problems and issues, see what people like and what not. People who complain about your website, products or services aren’t an annoyance, they’re actually helping you do a better job.

Analyzing visitors/customer feedback is important as it helps you not only get to know your visitors/customers better but also increase their engagement and loyalty. Feedback can help guide website, product and service development so you focus your resources on what your visitors/customers really want from you.

This plugin will help you to make the right changes to your website, products or services.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `nedwp-feedback-modal` to the `wp-content/plugins` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to Feedback Modal in admin area of your site, set it up to your needs.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I hide feedback modal in mobile devices ? =
Yes. You can select in which devices feedback modal will show up.

= Can I export feedback modal submissions ? =
Yes. You can export submissions data to CSV or JSON formats.

= Do I will be notified when a feedback was recived ? =
Yes. You can enable or disable email notifications for submissions.

= Does feedback modal have a responsive design ? =
Yes. Feedback modal is responsive and look good on all devices.

= Can feedback modal customized and styled ? =
Yes. A lot of options are available to edit as needed.
